Welcome to Text Summarizer!

A comprehensive text summarizer combines both extractive and abstractive techniques to deliver concise, relevant summaries. Extractive summarization selects key sentences directly from the source text based on scoring algorithms like TF-IDF and cosine similarity, ensuring the most important information is retained. Abstractive summarization, powered by advanced transformer models like BERT or T5, generates new sentences that rephrase and condense the original content, providing a coherent and fluid summary. This hybrid approach leverages the strengths of both methods, ensuring accuracy, context, and readability, making it an invaluable tool for efficiently digesting large volumes of text.
